Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

At Urkamso Bottling Company, we are committed to protecting your privacy. We collect certain personal data from our website visitors, clients, and partners to provide better services. This includes information such as your name, contact details, and business preferences when you interact with us.

1.1 Data Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, certain data is collected automatically, including your IP address, browser type, access times, and referring website. We also collect information about the pages you visit on our site. This helps us analyze how our site is used and improve its functionality.

1.2 Information You Provide

When you contact us for inquiries or sign up for a service, we collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and any additional details you provide. This information is used for business communications and to fulfill your requests.

2. How We Use Your Data

Urkamso Bottling Company uses your personal data to improve our services, streamline operations, and communicate with you effectively. Some of the ways we use your data include:

  • Providing the services you request, such as product information or quotations.
  • Processing orders, payments, and transactions securely.
  • Improving our website's performance and user experience based on analytical data.
  • Keeping you informed about our latest products, offers, and news (with your consent).

3. Data Security and Retention

Urkamso Bottling Company takes data security seriously. We implement various measures to ensure that your data is stored securely and accessed only by authorized personnel. Your personal information will be retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties unless it is necessary to fulfill your requests (e.g., processing payments) or required by law. Any third parties that handle your data on our behalf are required to maintain confidentiality and security.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data. If you have any concerns about how we use your data or wish to update your preferences, please contact us at info@urkamso.com.

Contact Us

For any privacy-related inquiries or concerns, feel free to reach out to us at info@urkamso.com. We are committed to addressing any questions you may have about our privacy practices.
